About the Company

Powering Philippine Industries with World-Class Pumps

Worthrand Petropower Corporation (WPC) was incorporated by a group of young Filipino entrepreneurs who have solid vision and optimism about harnessing and exploiting market opportunities both in local and global business playground. WPC was founded on March 1, 1999, registered with the Securities and Exchange Commission on April 28, 1999, and started official operations on May 5, 1999.

What We Offer

Our Products & Services

We supply a full range of Flowserve industrial pump solutions backed by technical expertise and after-sales service.

⚙️
Industrial Process Pumps

High-performance centrifugal and positive displacement pumps for demanding industrial process applications in chemical, petrochemical, and refinery plants.

🛢️
Oil & Gas Pumps

Specialized pump solutions engineered for upstream, midstream, and downstream oil and gas operations — built for reliability in the harshest environments.

🧪
Chemical Pumps

Corrosion-resistant pump systems designed for safe and efficient handling of acids, solvents, and other aggressive chemical fluids.

⚡
Gear Pumps

Precision gear pump solutions for viscous fluid transfer, lubrication systems, fuel handling, and hydraulic applications across multiple industries.

Marine Pump Supply

Certified marine-grade pump equipment and spare parts for vessels docking at Manila, Clark, and other Philippine ports — ready for urgent delivery.

Spare Parts & After-Sales

Genuine Flowserve spare parts and technical after-sales support to ensure your pump systems operate at peak performance at all times.
